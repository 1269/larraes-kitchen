HERO-05 enforcement notes:
- Scrim uses `from-ink/72 via-ink/35 to-transparent` — darkens bottom ~55% where content sits, preserves photo visibility up top.
- Scrim is `aria-hidden` (decorative).
- Content block is `justify-end` so headline + subheadline + CTA sit in the dark region. `text-white/90` on subheadline preserves contrast even with lighter scrim.
- UI-SPEC § Color requires 4.5:1 minimum per-photo; if a future hero photo is swapped in and breaks contrast, author's responsibility (CI does not catch per UI-SPEC note).

PERF-04 enforcement notes:
- `loading="eager"` + `decoding="async"` — eager loads immediately (hero LCP element). Other sections' images use `loading="lazy"` exclusively (enforced in their acceptance criteria).
- `width={2560} height={1440}` provides intrinsic dimensions; Astro's `<Image>` generates responsive srcset from `widths` and respects `sizes`.

Anti-patterns (from PATTERNS.md § HeroSection):
- Using raw `<img>` (loses AVIF/WebP).
- Omitting `min-h-[640px]` (very-tall browsers push CTA off-screen).
- Placing scrim above content in the z-stack.
- Omitting the focus-visible ring on the CTA (especially over the photo — use `outline-white`).
