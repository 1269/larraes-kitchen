# UX Strategy Brief - Larrae's Kitchen

## User Personas (Prioritized)

### 1. Family Celebration Cynthia
**Demographics:**
- 42-year-old professional mother of two
- Homeowner in Benicia or surrounding areas
- Household income: $120,000+
- Values family traditions and quality experiences

**Goals:**
- Find a reliable catering service for family gatherings (10-20 people)
- Serve authentic soul food that creates memorable experiences
- Support local, Black-owned businesses with cultural heritage
- Get clear pricing without hidden fees

**Pain Points:**
- Limited time to research catering options
- Previous disappointing experiences with generic catering
- Wants authentic soul food but can't find specialized options in Benicia
- Needs responsive communication for planning events

### 2. Social Host Ethan
**Demographics:**
- 52-year-old professional with established social network
- Frequently hosts dinner parties and social gatherings
- Homeowner in upscale Benicia neighborhood
- Appreciates authentic culinary experiences

**Goals:**
- Impress guests with distinctive, culturally-rich food options
- Create conversation-worthy dining experiences
- Support diverse local businesses
- Build relationship with quality food providers

**Pain Points:**
- Has exhausted standard catering options in Benicia
- Needs flexible ordering for varying group sizes (typically 20-30 people)
- Wants food with a story and cultural significance
- Requires reliability for important social occasions

### 3. Corporate Planner Emma
**Demographics:**
- 36-year-old event coordinator for mid-sized company
- Responsible for quarterly company events
- Based in Benicia with regional responsibilities
- Budget-conscious but authorized to make decisions

**Goals:**
- Find unique catering options that stand out from typical corporate fare
- Secure reliable, professional service for larger functions (50-75 people)
- Stay within budget while offering quality experience
- Receive positive feedback from attendees about food quality

**Pain Points:**
- Tired of standard corporate catering options
- Needs clear documentation for expense reporting
- Requires professional presentation for corporate environment
- Must ensure timely delivery and setup for business functions

## User Journey Map: Residential Client

1. **Awareness Stage**
   - Discovers Larrae's Kitchen through local Facebook group or Instagram post
   - Views compelling food imagery showing authentic soul food dishes
   - Notices "Authentic Soul Food Catering in Benicia" tagline
   - Clicks through to website from social profile

2. **Consideration Stage**
   - Immediately sees high-impact hero image of signature soul food dishes
   - Reads compelling story about chef's connection to soul food heritage
   - Browses menu section focusing on authentic dishes like collard greens, mac and cheese
   - Compares package options focusing on Small Gathering Package (10-20 people)
   - Views gallery showing previous family gatherings and food presentation

3. **Decision Stage**
   - Reviews testimonials from similar residential clients
   - Checks FAQ for ordering timeframe and delivery options
   - Evaluates pricing for budget alignment ($18-22/person range)
   - Completes inquiry form with specific event details
   - Expects prompt follow-up to discuss menu customization

4. **Post-Conversion**
   - Receives personalized proposal with menu options
   - Makes deposit payment to secure date
   - Gets pre-event communication confirming details
   - Experiences catering service with authentic soul food
   - Shares experience via social media with food photos
   - Receives follow-up for feedback and future events

## Content Strategy Recommendations

### Content Principles
- **Cultural Authenticity**: Emphasize soul food heritage and tradition
- **Visual Storytelling**: Use high-quality food imagery to convey flavor and quality
- **Clear Value Proposition**: Highlight position as Benicia's authentic soul food specialist
- **Transparent Pricing**: Make package pricing clear and upfront
- **Emotional Connection**: Tell the story behind the food and chef's heritage

### Key Content by Section

**Hero Section:**
- Headline: "Authentic Soul Food Catering in Benicia"
- Subheading: "Bringing Southern Heritage to Your Table"
- Hero image featuring signature soul food spread
- CTA Button: "Get a Quote" or "Book Your Event"

**About Section:**
- Origin story connecting chef to soul food traditions
- Personal connection to recipes and cooking methods
- Emphasis on being Benicia's only dedicated soul food caterer
- Supporting imagery showing chef/team preparing food

**Menu Section:**
- Authentic menu items categorized by proteins, sides, and desserts
- Descriptions emphasizing traditional preparation methods
- "Family recipes passed down through generations" messaging
- Dietary indicators for vegetarian options

**Catering Packages:**
- Clear, transparent tiered pricing matching market research:
  - Small Gathering: $18-22/person (10-20 people)
  - Medium Event: $16-20/person (21-30 people)
  - Large Gathering: $15-18/person (50-75 people)
- Package comparisons highlighting inclusions
- Add-on options for desserts and additional proteins

**Gallery Section:**
- High-quality images of actual catered events
- Focus on food presentation and happy guests
- Balanced representation of different event types
- Captions highlighting event type and package used

**Testimonials Section:**
- Authentic testimonials from prior clients
- Emphasis on food quality, authenticity, and service
- Representation from all target audience segments
- Star ratings for visual credibility

## Key Performance Indicators

- **Inquiry Form Submission Rate**: Target 5%+ of total visitors
- **Menu/Package View Duration**: Average 1+ minute on package section
- **Gallery Engagement**: 40%+ of visitors interact with gallery

- **Average Session Duration**: Target 2+ minutes
- **Return Visitor Rate**: 25%+ by month 3
- **Social Media Referral Traffic**: 30%+ of total traffic
- **Email Newsletter Signup Rate**: 3%+ of visitors

### Business Impact KPIs
- **New Client Acquisition**: 5+ per month goal
- **Average Order Value**: $500+ per event
- **Repeat Business Rate**: 20%+ by month 6
- **Geographical Distribution**: 80% Benicia, 20% surrounding areas
